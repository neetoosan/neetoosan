My Story

From curiosity to code • One bug at a time

I'm Israel Oyekanmi a naturally curious creative who stumbled into code and never looked back. My journey started with a simple question: "What's harder to understand than a lady?" Someone said Python. I laughed, then I tried it. Turns out, they weren't entirely wrong but I was hooked. 🐍

🐍 Python → 🎮 C++ Games → 🖥️ PySide6 → 🚀 Flet → 💰 Sold Apps

What began as curiosity quickly became obsession. I dove into Python, then wandered into game development with C++, only to discover I could build real desktop applications with it. That led me to PySide6, where I started crafting tools that actually solved problems. But I wanted more I wanted my work to reach everywhere. That's when I found Flutter -> Flet, and everything clicked. Cross-platform apps, one codebase, endless possibilities.

t The road wasn't smooth. I pitched to schools, students, and organizations and heard "no" more times than I can count. The feedback was brutal, the rejections stung. But every "no" fueled me instead of breaking me.

Eventually, I started selling my applications. Not because it was easy because I refused to quit. Today, I build software that works in the real world: backend systems that power operations, automation tools that save hours, and applications that make people's lives easier. I bring the same curiosity that got me started to every project always learning, always improving, always asking "what if?"

💡
